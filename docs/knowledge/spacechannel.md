# Space Channel — site overview

Space Channel (spacechannel.com) is a mission-focused space-intelligence
dashboard styled as a terminal ("SPACE CHANNEL > TERMINAL"). It presents live
and daily-updated widgets about spaceflight, astronomy, and UFO/UAP topics,
plus LIVE TV and RADIO streams, in Dark Cockpit or Daylight themes.

Main sections and widgets (from the site's navigation):

- Anomaly Division — UFO & UAP coverage: a daily UFO & UAP news wire, the
  "UFO Files Released" archive, and AI-panel debates of historic UFO cases.
- Launch Command — upcoming rocket launches and a Mission Tracker.
- Deep Space Network — live DSN antenna activity monitor.
- Artemis Console — NASA Moon to Mars program tracking.
- ISS Operations — live International Space Station position.
- Lunar Tracking (lunar cycle), Mars Operations, and Missions & Sky.
- Cosmic Weather and Placements/Numerology, plus Solar Operations for space
  weather and a Sky Monitor / Horizon View.
- Exoplanet Catalog, Planet Catalog, and a 3D Planet Orrery.
- Webb Observatory — James Webb telescope blog feed.
- Intelligence Feed / Data Lens — curated space-industry news articles from
  outlets like SpaceNews, with a Daily Briefing and Intel Report.
- Sector Analysis — space-industry stocks and market trends.
- Orbital Traffic — satellite density and object tracking, with an Object
  Atlas image search.
- NetTrek Tactical Assault — a game in beta.
- Mission Supply — the site's shop.
- Music — featuring PLANETS, an improvised jazz-guitar journey through the
  planets performed by Grammy- and Emmy-nominated guitarist David Becker,
  with live tour dates listed on the site.
- Podcasts — MAXQ (space-sector markets and news) and UFO Files.

This overview describes the site's structure as of the latest crawl; features
may change. Detailed, current data for launches, news, cases, podcasts, tour
dates, and DSN status appears in the feed sections below.

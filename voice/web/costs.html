Where the live numbers come from

AUDITABLE USAGE · VERSIONED RATES

1 · Cost ledger

Each completed call writes raw units to cost_ledger: connected minutes, STT audio-seconds, provider token receipts, TTS characters, and infrastructure minutes. A ledger failure never interrupts a call.

2 · Versioned pricing

voice/pricing.json records model input/output/cache rates, telephony rates, and an as-of date. Call rows preserve the rate snapshot used for their auditable historical total.

3 · Privacy-safe aggregation

/api/costs joins completed-call metadata for business, flow, duration, and customer frequency. Caller numbers are HMAC-hashed before leaving the server and are never returned by this endpoint.

4 · Client-side what-if arithmetic

The scenario toggle and supervisor selector re-price this page without mutating the ledger, making realistic-load and 24×7 comparisons immediate.

Rates are versioned in voice/pricing.json. Realistic duty and model scenarios come from the scheduler-model-costs rate source; observed call usage comes only from the local metrics DB.
